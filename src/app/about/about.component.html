Then I cofounded and worked as a Graphic Designer with Dots where I was in charge of brand identity, photo retouching and web design for small business and management of social media for a high end mexican jewelry store, while studying my MBA.

In the meantime I was invited to teach at the Presidency of Mexico and in my former university as a Professor of Design.

I worked for Digital Arts Network and then Terán/TBWA as a Digital Art Director, where I created and designed digital concepts, strategies and focused in content art direction (animations, case studies, banners, ads, posts) and web design, for PepsiCo Mexico, Mead Johnson, EoS, WalMart,INFINITI and TBWA itself. Being the youngest member in my team I developed my skills from working directly with senior designers, senior copywriters and SM managers.

When life gave me lemons, I moved to New York City!
